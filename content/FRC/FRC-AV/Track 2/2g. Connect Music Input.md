1. Retrieve the AV laptop from the Audio [[FRC/FRC-AV/index#Equipment Locations and Storage Descriptions|AV tote]].![[FRC/FRC-AV/Track 2/Media/1000014504.webp]]
2. Retrieve a headphone cable, also from the audio AV tote. %%TODO: headphone cable is in av tote or in drawers?%%
3. Connect the headphone output of the laptop to the "3.5mm Audio In" jack on the Streaming Unit.
